# Digital Asset Broker Reporting FAQ — Relevant 2025 Notes

## Source Pointer

- IRS broker-reporting FAQ for digital assets, updated in 2025

## Key Operational Points

- `Form 1099-DA` reporting begins for covered transactions on or after
  `2025-01-01`.
- Customer-provided acquisition information from a transferring broker can be
  used for lot ordering when it is reasonably reliable.
- That same customer-provided acquisition information cannot be used by the
  reporting broker as basis reporting support on `Form 1099-DA`.
- When multiple transferred-in lots are sold in one transaction, the 2025
  instructions may permit one `Form 1099-DA`; if transfer-in dates vary, the
  transfer-in date box may be left blank.

## Why This Matters For The Agent

- Transfer history may help identify lots, but it does not eliminate the need
  for defensible basis support on the taxpayer's return.
