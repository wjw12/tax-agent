# 2025 Form 1040 / 1040-SR Instructions — Relevant What's New Notes

## Source Pointer

- IRS instructions: `i1040gi` for tax year 2025

## Schedule 1-A

- Tax year 2025 adds `Schedule 1-A (Form 1040)`.
- The four new deductions are:
  - qualified tips
  - qualified overtime compensation
  - qualified passenger vehicle loan interest
  - enhanced deduction for seniors
- These deductions are available whether the taxpayer takes the standard
  deduction or itemizes.

## Digital Assets

- Broker reporting on `Form 1099-DA` begins in 2025.
- Basis reporting on `Form 1099-DA` is optional for 2025.
- Taxpayers must still answer the digital-asset question and report gain or
  loss even if basis was not reported.

## Child Credits

- For tax year 2025, the maximum `CTC` is `2,200` dollars per qualifying child.
- Up to `1,700` dollars may be claimed as `ACTC`.
- Beginning in 2025, `CTC` and `ACTC` require a valid employment-authorized
  `SSN` issued on or before the return due date, including extensions.
- On a joint return, one spouse must have a valid `SSN`; the other spouse must
  have either an `SSN` or `ITIN` issued on or before the due date.

## 1099-K

- For tax year 2025, `Form 1099-K` reporting applies only when business
  transactions exceed `20,000` dollars and `200` transactions.

## Business And Itemized Deltas

- Domestic research and experimental expenditures become deductible again
  beginning in 2025, with a capitalization or amortization option.
- The `SALT` cap increased for 2025.

## Detect-And-Escalate Topics

- `Form 4547` for Trump accounts
- `Form 1062` for farmland-sale installment elections
- 2025 adoption-credit changes tied to `Form 8839`
