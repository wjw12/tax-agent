# Form 8829 — 2025 What's New Notes

## Source Pointer

- IRS instructions: `Form 8829` for tax year 2025

## Key 2025 Change

- The overall `SALT` deduction limit increased to `40,000` dollars
  (`20,000` if married filing separately), with the 2025 phase-down floor
  described in the instructions.

## Carryover Reminder

- If the taxpayer used the simplified method for 2024 but not for 2025,
  prior-year unallowed expenses may carry to the 2025 `Form 8829`.
