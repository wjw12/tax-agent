# Form 1040-NR — 2025 What's New Notes

## Source Pointer

- IRS instructions: `Form 1040-NR` for tax year 2025

## Key 2025 Changes

- `Form 1040-NR` picks up the new `Schedule 1-A` flow for 2025.
- The 2025 deductions highlighted for `1040-NR` include qualified tips,
  qualified overtime compensation, and the enhanced senior deduction.
- `Form 1099-DA` reporting begins in 2025, with optional basis reporting.
- `Form 1099-K` reporting stays at the `20,000` dollar and `200` transaction
  business threshold for 2025.
- The 2025 child-credit identity rules also affect the nonresident path when
  those credits are otherwise in play.

## Detect-And-Escalate Topics

- `Form 4547` for Trump accounts
- `Form 1062` for farmland-sale installment elections
- 2025 adoption-credit changes tied to `Form 8839`
