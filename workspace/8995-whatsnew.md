# Form 8995 / 8995-A — 2025 What's New Notes

## Source Pointer

- IRS instructions: `Form 8995` and `Form 8995-A` for tax year 2025

## Key 2025 Change

- Tip income excluded under section `224` is also excluded from `QBI`.

## Ongoing QBI Reminder

- `QBI` is based on how the item is treated on the federal return, not just how
  it appears on `Schedule C`, `Schedule K-1`, or `Form 1099-PATR`.
- Items do not become `QBI` automatically just because they appear on a
  business statement.
