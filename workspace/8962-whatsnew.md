# Form 8962 — 2025 What's New Notes

## Source Pointer

- IRS instructions: `Form 8962` for tax year 2025

## Key 2025 Changes

- Beginning `2025-01-01`, a month can still count as a coverage month if part
  of the premium was unpaid but enough was paid to avoid termination under the
  instruction rules.
- In some cases, reporting entities are no longer required to mail `Forms 1095-B`
  and `1095-C` automatically unless the taxpayer requests them.

## Ongoing Marketplace Reminder

- `Marketplace` coverage still relies on `Form 1095-A` and `Form 8962`.
