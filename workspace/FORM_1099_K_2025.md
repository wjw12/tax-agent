# Form 1099-K — 2025 Handling Rules

Use this file when the case includes `Form 1099-K`, payment-platform volume, or
taxpayer confusion about platform gross receipts.

## Core Policy

Do not treat `Form 1099-K` gross volume as automatic taxable business income.

For tax year 2025, the IRS states that `Form 1099-K` is issued only when
business transactions exceed `20,000` dollars and `200` transactions.
The form's presence or absence does not decide taxability.

## Routing Rules

- If the form reflects business receipts, reconcile it against books, ledgers,
  and other payer statements before treating it as `Schedule C` gross income.
- If the form is incorrect or reflects personal items sold at a loss, back the
  amount out through the entry space on `Schedule 1`.
- If the transaction produced a real gain, route that gain through `Form 8949`
  and `Schedule D`.

## Review Rule

- Check for double counting with `1099-NEC`, card-processor reports, merchant
  statements, and business ledgers.
