# Form 4562 — 2025 What's New Notes

## Source Pointer

- IRS instructions: `Form 4562` for tax year 2025

## Key 2025 Changes

- `Form 4562` adds new lines `19h` and `20e` for `50`-year property.
- Line `23` was split into `23a` and `23b` for section `263A` capitalized cost
  detail.
- Part `V` adds line `24c` for aircraft depreciation reporting.

## Depreciation Rules

- Certain qualified property acquired and placed in service after
  `2025-01-19` again qualifies for `100%` bonus depreciation.
- Certain property acquired before `2025-01-20` remains subject to the
  `40%` or `60%` phase-down rules described in the instructions.
- Qualified production property may get a separate `100%` allowance when the
  2025 conditions in the instructions are met.

## Research And Experimental Expenditures

- New section `174A` allows current deduction of domestic research and
  experimental expenditures beginning in 2025.
- Taxpayers can instead elect capitalization and amortization.
- Foreign research still must be capitalized and amortized over the longer
  period described in the instructions.
