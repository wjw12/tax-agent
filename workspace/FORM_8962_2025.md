# Form 8962 / Marketplace Coverage — 2025 Rules

Use this file when the case includes `Marketplace` coverage, `Form 1095-A`, or
`Form 8962`.

## 2025 Coverage-Month Rule

Beginning `2025-01-01`, a month can still count as a coverage month even if
premiums were only partially paid, as long as enough was paid to avoid
termination of coverage under the applicable rule.

Use the 2024 federal poverty line tables referenced by the 2025
`Form 8962` instructions. Keep the actual table values in retrieval or config,
not in prompt prose. The structured config hook for this is
[tax_constants_2025.py](/home/appuser/tax/src/tax_constants_2025.py).

## Document-Request Rule

Do not mark the case incomplete only because the taxpayer did not receive
`Form 1095-B` or `Form 1095-C` in the mail.

For tax year 2025, some reporting entities are no longer required to mail those
forms automatically unless requested.

Marketplace coverage still depends on `Form 1095-A` and `Form 8962`.
