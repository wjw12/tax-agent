# Understanding Form 1099-DA — Relevant 2025 Notes

## Source Pointer

- IRS taxpayer guidance on `Form 1099-DA`

## Key 2025 Points

- `Form 1099-DA` reports gross proceeds and sometimes basis for digital-asset
  dispositions.
- Taxpayers must report digital-asset income, gain, and loss whether or not
  they receive the form.
- For tax year 2025, the filing requirement generally applies to U.S. brokers,
  so taxpayers using foreign brokers may not receive `Form 1099-DA`.
- Basis still must be calculated before filing.

## Correction Rule

- If `Form 1099-DA` is wrong, the taxpayer should request a corrected form from
  the issuer and keep the correspondence, but should not wait indefinitely to
  file.
