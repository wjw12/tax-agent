# Schedule C / 4562 / 8829 / 8995 — 2025 Delta Rules

Use this file when the case includes:

- `Schedule C`
- `Form 4562`
- `Form 8829`
- `Form 8995` or `Form 8995-A`
- mixed-use vehicle or home-office issues tied to those forms

Do not restate generic business-expense law here.
Use this file only for the 2025 deltas that are easy to miss if the model falls
back to older patterns.

## 2025 Depreciation And Research Rules

- `100%` bonus depreciation is back for certain qualified property acquired and
  placed in service after `2025-01-19`.
- `Form 4562` changed for the 2025 depreciation rules.
- Domestic research and experimental expenditures become deductible again
  beginning in 2025, with an election to capitalize or amortize instead.

## 2025 QBI Rule

- Use `Form 8995` only when taxable income before the `QBI` deduction is at or
  below `394,600` dollars for `MFJ` or `197,300` dollars for all other filing
  statuses. Above that, route to `Form 8995-A`.
- Excludable qualified tip income under section `224` is excluded from `QBI`
  for `Form 8995` and `Form 8995-A`.

## 2025 Mileage Rule

- The standard business mileage rate is `70` cents per mile for tax year 2025.

## Mixed-Use Vehicle And Home-Office Routing

- The personal-use share of passenger-vehicle loan interest belongs on
  `Schedule 1-A`, not on `Schedule C`.
- The business-use share may remain on `Schedule C`, `Schedule E`, or
  `Schedule F` if otherwise deductible.
- `Schedule A` and `Form 8829` analysis must use the 2025 `SALT` cap rules,
  not older default caps.
