# Schedule C (Form 1040) — 2025 What's New Notes

## Source Pointer

- IRS instructions: `Schedule C (Form 1040)` for tax year 2025

## Key 2025 Deltas

- The standard mileage rate is `70` cents per mile.
- Certain qualified property acquired and placed in service after
  `2025-01-19` can take `100%` bonus depreciation.
- Domestic research and experimental expenditures become currently deductible
  again in 2025, with a capitalization or amortization election.

## New Schedule 1-A Interactions

- Qualified tips are deducted on `Schedule 1-A`, not on `Schedule C`.
- Qualified overtime compensation is deducted on `Schedule 1-A`, not on
  `Schedule C`.
- If a mixed-use vehicle loan qualifies, the personal-use share belongs on
  `Schedule 1-A`; only the business-use share can remain on `Schedule C`.
